BITM Campus

BASIS Institute of Technology & Management (BITM) was established with a vision to be world-class IT institute in Bangladesh for the purpose of enhancing the competitiveness of the IT Sector in Bangladesh by creating a pool of qualified IT professionals and quality certified IT companies. World Bank, through support to Hi-tech park project, has been providing support in the areas of infrastructure development, organizational development and training activities.

To address the skill gap of HR in the industry, BASIS started its own training activities in 2007 . Later in 2012, BASIS institutionalized its training activities and set up BASIS Institute of Technology & Management (BITM) with the support of World Bank. BITM was established with a vision to be a world-class IT institute in Bangladesh for the purpose of enhancing the competitiveness of the IT Sector in Bangladesh by creating a pool of qualified IT professionals and quality certified IT companies.

Capacity of BITM :
At present, BITM has a capacity • To train over 2,000 professionals (both entry level & management level) • To run parallel courses in 4 equipped lab • 50+ industry professionals involved with BITM to provide the world class training
